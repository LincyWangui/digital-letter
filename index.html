Open Letter

Dear Johnson

Some letters are written in ink, others in memory. This one lives somewhere in between.


Every photo here is a moment, every word a reminder that small things become big stories when we look back.


Your mind.Your passion and hardwork in aviaton translating to brilliance, certificates left on your table, all straight As. Growing up your dedication has been like a mirror into what true success is built on. All while seemingly effortless at times, as you loved to study seated up on your bed. Your creativity unmatched as you aways had new ways of preparing meals


Your heart. The many surprises and unceremonius gifts you loved to get for us just to make us smile, not to mention cutting treats in half, held behind your back, so we would choose fairly. The laughter during late night board games you insisted we take a part in although late just so we wouldnt miss out. Many of our fondest childhood memories have you written into them. Your love and consideration never went unnoticed.


From your Sisters
