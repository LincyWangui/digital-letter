Dear Johnson

Some letters are written in ink, others in memory. This one lives somewhere in between.

Every photo here is a moment, every word a reminder that small things become big stories when we look back.

Your mind. Your passion and hard work in aviation translating to brilliance, certificates left on your table, all straight As. Growing up, your dedication has been like a mirror into what true success is built on. All while seeming effortless at times, as you loved to study seated up on your bed. Your creativity unmatched as you always had new ways of preparing meals.

Your heart. The many surprises and unceremonious gifts you loved to get for us just to make us smile, not to mention cutting treats in half, held behind your back, so we would choose fairly. The laughter during late night board games you insisted we take part in, although late, just so we would not miss out. Many of our fondest childhood memories have you written into them. Your love and consideration never went unnoticed.

The Man You Are

These moments reflect your character, your presence, your brilliance. They capture the kind of person you are.

The Love Around You

Here lives the love that has always surrounded you - in family, in togetherness.

Some people leave their goodness everywhere they have been. This is only a small attempt to gather a little of yours in one place.
